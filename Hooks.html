Hooks

  • Mayor Elira Thorne Adventure
    • Clear Caves:

      Caves beyond the mines filled with creatures that have become too dangerous clear them out.

      Reward: 35gp for every cavern cleared

      One cave will connect with cavern near mine shaft.

      One cave has bandits making “Hollowdust”

      Source: Keep on the Borderlands

    • Rumors of Hollowdust:

      There are rumors of Hollowdust problems down stream.

  • Selene Veyra Adventure
    • Protect Caravan:

      Protect a caravan with a silver shipment to the right. Less frequent trade route.

      Reward: 200gp

      Protect from bandits or wild beasts.

      Maybe unwhittingly smuggling “Hollowdust”

  • Toma Reed Adventure
    • Forage in Forest:

      Toma is studying the spirits in this region. Focusing on the old willow tree.

      • Moonpetal Blossoms – Silver-white flowers that only open under the full moon. Toma believes their petals “hold” the moon’s reflection, amplifying divination magic. Challenge: Must find a grove under moonlight, likely protected by fey or nocturnal predators.
      • Whisperbark Chips – Strips of bark from trees that “speak” when the wind passes through their hollow knots. Used in rituals of communication. Challenge: Dangerous climbing to harvest safely without angering the tree’s spirit.
      • Ironcap Mushrooms – Hardy, dark fungi growing in shadowed caves, resilient to decay. Their essence may help Toma stabilize echo fluctuations. Challenge: Found near caves that border the corrupted mushroom spirit’s influence.
      • Stardew Fern Fronds – Ferns that glow faintly with dew after meteor showers. Believed to carry celestial resonance, important for scrying attempts. Challenge: Requires venturing to remote glades at night, potentially stalked by forest spirits who dislike trespassers.
