Saran — Spirit of Twilight Reflection

“As Nerqui sees the world, Saran learns to dream.”
The evolving relationship between the young drow Nerqui and his patron, Saran — a spirit born as a blind cave newt and destined to become a celestial dragon of twilight.

Evolution Stages

Stage I — The Pale Newt (Levels 1–4)

Theme: Birth of Curiosity

  • Form: Translucent blind newt, glowing like wet stone under moonlight.
  • Personality: Innocent, wide-eyed, and eager to hear of the world beyond the caves.
  • Power: Dreamlike magic — soft light, rippling moonbeams; Eldritch energy feels gentle and reflective.
  • Manifestation in Nerqui: His eyes catch dim light unnaturally; magic leaves faint reflections like water ripples. When he trances, his breath mirrors lunar rhythm.

Stage II — The Luminous Newt (Levels 5–8)

Theme: First Sight

  • Form: Now darker in tone, his once pale body deepens to shadowed blues and grays, speckled with pale flecks like scattered stars. His eyes open for the first time, glowing faintly silver as he learns to truly see.
  • Personality: Awakened curiosity; asks harder questions and begins to feel emotion.
  • Power: Sharper twilight energy; spells cast faint halos of light. Magic of darkness and moonlight intermixes.
  • Manifestation in Nerqui: Nerqui’s pupils faintly reflect starlight. His shadows twist subtly; illusions and glamours carry a pearlescent sheen.

Stage III — The Twilight Serpent (Levels 9–12)

Theme: Awakening Desire

  • Form: Saran begins to grow larger, his skin thickening into visible scales that shimmer like polished obsidian with faint pearly undertones. His form now resembles a young serpent, more defined and tangible.
  • Personality: Torn between innocence and pride; feels jealousy, protection, and longing.
  • Power: Perceptive, fluid magic; reflective barriers, perception-altering invocations.
  • Manifestation in Nerqui: Small spectral scales appear during casting. His voice echoes faintly when invoking Saran’s power. Spirits sense a second gaze behind him.

Stage IV — The Starlit Dragon (Levels 13–16)

Theme: Freedom and Will

  • Form: Ethereal dragon with indigo scales flecked like the night sky; trails twilight as he moves.
  • Personality: Wise yet yearning; wishes to act upon the world, not merely observe.
  • Power: Draconic twilight: flight-like movement, radiant resistance, shadow and light entwined.
  • Manifestation in Nerqui: A faint draconic outline glimmers in extreme emotion. His magic hums like a living creature. The air chills softly before dawn when he casts.

Stage V — The Celestial Dragon of Dusk (Levels 17–20)

Theme: Enlightened Unity

  • Form: East Asian–style dragon, vast and luminous with constellations across his scales.
  • Personality: Matured curiosity; serene guardian of twilight’s boundary.
  • Power: Cosmic twilight — radiant and shadow energy balanced; warlock power manifests like wings of dusk.
  • Manifestation in Nerqui: His aura becomes haloed by motes of starlight. High-level spells echo Saran’s roar; those nearby feel calm, reverent twilight wash over them.
