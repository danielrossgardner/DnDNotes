  • Hollowdust

    A pale-gray, powdery substance ground from corrupted mushrooms cultivated in hidden caves beyond the Silverstream Mines.

    • Appearance & Use:

      A pale-gray, powdery substance ground from corrupted mushrooms in the caverns beyond the Silverstream Mines. It has a faint, earthy glow in moonlight. Usually inhaled through a reed tube or mixed into wine. Carried in tiny clay vials or wrapped in waxed paper.

    • Effects (Game Mechanics):
      • Immediate Effect: Gain advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks and initiative rolls for 1 hour.
      • Lingering Side Effect: At the end of the duration, the user must succeed on a DC 12 Constitution saving throw or gain 1 level of exhaustion.
      • Addiction Risk:

        If a creature uses Hollowdust more than once within a 7-day period, they must succeed on a DC 14 Wisdom saving throw or develop dependency. Dependency means the user suffers disadvantage on all Wisdom saving throws and ability checks until they take another dose.

    • Corruption Taint:

      Long-term users may develop hallucinations tied to the Mushroom Spirit (Death Domain), hearing whispers or seeing fungal growths where none exist. Some believe frequent users eventually attract the spirit’s attention, becoming thralls or spore-ridden husks.
